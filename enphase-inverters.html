Enphase Microinverters – Smart, Efficient, and Reliable

About Enphase:
Enphase is a leading provider of microinverter-based solar systems, offering high efficiency, simplified installation, and advanced monitoring. Their IQ Series microinverters are smart-grid ready and designed for both residential and commercial systems. Enphase microinverters are tested extensively, with over a million hours of power-on testing, supporting an industry-leading 25-year warranty.

Key Highlights

  • High Efficiency: Up to 97% efficiency, optimized for 60-cell and 72-cell modules.

  • Easy Installation: Lightweight microinverters with simplified two-wire cabling.

  • Smart Grid Ready: Complies with advanced grid support, voltage/frequency ride-through, and CA Rule 21 (UL 1741-SA).

  • Reliable: Class II double-insulated enclosure, UL listed, and over a million hours of testing.

  • Advanced Monitoring: Integrates with Enphase IQ Envoy, IQ Battery, and Enlighten monitoring software.

  • Backup & Energy Management: Ensemble technology enables grid-independent energy supply and backup power.

Types of Enphase Inverters

Series Application Key Features
IQ 7 / IQ 7+ Residential Supports 60-cell & 72-cell modules, high efficiency, advanced monitoring, lightweight, fast installation
IQ 6 / IQ 6+ Residential Earlier generation, compatible with 60-cell modules, improved energy harvest and uptime
IQ 7X Residential/Commercial Optimized for high-power 96-cell modules, high efficiency, reliable performance
IQ Commercial Commercial Handles higher power & voltage, designed for larger systems, smart monitoring
IQ Ensemble / IQ 8 / IQ 8D Residential & Commercial Energy management system with backup power, grid-independent operation, advanced energy optimization

Featured Model – ENPHASE IQ 7A Micro (ENP-IQ7A)

  • Peak Output: 366 VA @ 240 VAC, 295 VA @ 208 VAC

  • Efficiency: Highest system efficiency up to 97%

  • Installation: Lightweight, fast installation with two-wire cabling

  • Safety: Built-in rapid shutdown (NEC 2014 & 2017), Class II double-insulated enclosure, UL listed

  • Smart Grid Ready: Advanced grid support, voltage & frequency ride-through

  • Monitoring: Requires Envoy + internet connection, integrates with Enlighten software

Why Choose Enphase Microinverters

  • Simplifies installation for residential and commercial solar systems

  • Modular approach: Each panel has its own inverter, maximizing energy harvest even if some panels are shaded or dirty

  • Industry-leading reliability and 25-year warranty

  • Smart energy management and grid-independent operation via Ensemble technology
