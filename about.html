About Radiant Power

Helping Australian homes and businesses transition to clean, reliable, and cost-effective solar energy.

28+ Years of expertise
Who We Are

Powering a Smarter Energy Future

We are dedicated to helping homes and businesses transition to clean, reliable, and cost-effective solar energy. Our goal is to make renewable energy simple and accessible while helping our customers reduce electricity costs and move toward a more sustainable future.

By combining industry expertise with high-quality technology, we deliver solar solutions designed to perform efficiently and reliably for years to come.

How We Work

Experience You Can Trust. Service You Can Count On.

From your first conversation to decades of reliable energy production, Radiant Power brings deep industry expertise, end-to-end solar solutions, and a commitment to making clean energy simple.

Our Experience

Decades of Hands-On Solar Expertise

Our team has extensive experience across the full solar lifecycle — from system design and planning to installation and ongoing support. We focus on delivering solutions tailored to each property, ensuring optimal energy generation and long-term value.

We work closely with every client to understand their energy usage, goals, and property requirements before recommending the most suitable solar solution.

  • End-to-end system design & planning
  • Property-specific energy assessments
  • Professional installation & long-term support
