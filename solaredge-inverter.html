Why Choose SolarEdge?

More Energy From Every Panel

With Power Optimisers on each panel, SolarEdge systems minimise losses caused by shading, dirt, or panel mismatch, ensuring your system produces more energy over its lifetime—translating to higher savings.

Future-Ready System

SolarEdge inverters support battery backup, EV charging, Virtual Power Plant (VPP) integration, and panel expansion, making your system ready for tomorrow’s energy needs.

One Inverter For Any Home

Whether your installation has 10 panels or 30 panels, the same inverter can manage your system efficiently, offering flexibility and scalability.

Smarter Monitoring

Gain real-time insights into the performance of each panel with the mySolarEdge app, allowing easy monitoring, troubleshooting, and optimisation.

Longer Warranty

  • Inverter Warranty: 12 years (standard)

  • Power Optimiser Warranty: 25 years
    Providing long-term reliability and peace of mind.

See Your Savings

See exactly what’s happening on your roof, right from your phone. Manage your battery, EV charger, and smart devices to maximize energy use and get the most out of your solar system.
