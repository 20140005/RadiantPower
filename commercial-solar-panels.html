Commercial Solar

Power Your Business with Clean Energy

Commercial solar power systems offer significant economic advantages for businesses. By investing in solar energy, companies can lower operating costs while strengthening their sustainability credentials and enhancing their public image.

With electricity prices in Australia rising sharply in recent years, solar provides a smart and reliable way to protect your business from escalating energy costs while powering operations with clean, renewable energy.

Why Solar?

  • Generate your own clean electricity
  • Save up to 80% or more on electricity bills*
  • Reduce dependence on the power grid
  • Take advantage of government rebates and incentives

Return on Investment

With record-high rebates and rising feed-in tariffs, solar has become one of the smartest investments for businesses today.

When designed and installed correctly, a Radiant Power solar system can deliver returns of 28% or more. Flexible green loan options are also available, allowing eligible businesses to get started with $0 upfront.
