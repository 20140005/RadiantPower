SAJ Solar Inverters – Reliable, High-Performance Solutions for Australia

About SAJ:
Founded in 2005, SAJ is a leading solar inverter manufacturer with a strong presence in Australia. SAJ provides reliable residential and commercial solar solutions, integrating cutting-edge technology with robust performance.

Product Range:

1. Single-Phase Inverters (R5 Series):

  • Capacities: 3kW – 5kW

  • Dual MPPT trackers for optimal energy harvesting

  • Fanless design for silent operation

  • Maximum efficiency: 98.1%

2. Three-Phase Inverters (R6 Series):

  • Capacities: 5kW – 10kW

  • High string current support (up to 16A)

  • Built-in AC/DC surge protection

  • Maximum efficiency: 98.8%

3. Hybrid Inverters (H2 Series):

  • Integrates solar and energy storage for maximum self-consumption

  • Single-phase (5kW) and three-phase (5kW – 10kW) models

  • Supports fast battery charging/discharging and multiple smart modes

Key Features Across SAJ Inverters:

  • High efficiency (up to 98%) for maximum energy output

  • UPS functionality for seamless backup (switching ≤10ms)

  • Compatible with LiFePO4 batteries for long-lasting energy storage

  • Flexible DC oversizing (up to 200%) and high voltage ranges

  • Intelligent working modes with advanced protections: reverse polarity, overload, AC/DC surge, optional AFCI

  • Remote monitoring and control via Wi-Fi, Ethernet, 4G, and mobile app

  • Natural convection cooling for quiet operation (<35dBA)

  • IP65 rated for outdoor durability and wide temperature operation (-40°C to +60°C)

Warranty Options:

  • Flexible warranty from 5 to 20 years depending on model

Why Choose SAJ:
SAJ inverters combine high efficiency, smart backup capabilities, and future-ready technology, making them ideal for Australian residential and commercial solar systems.
