EGing Solar Panels

EGing is China’s first pure solar module manufacturer listed on the Shanghai A-share stock market. As a high-tech company, EGing specializes in the research, development, manufacturing, and sales of photovoltaic products.

With a vertically integrated system covering ingot, wafer, cell, module packaging, and solar power generation, EGing has an annual production capacity of 2GW for both mono- and polycrystalline solar modules.

EGing Solar Panels

EGing is a leading Chinese solar module manufacturer with vertically integrated production, specializing in high-performance photovoltaic solutions. EGing panels are engineered for reliability, durability, and optimal energy production.

Types of EGing Solar Panels

Monocrystalline Panels

  • Made from a single crystal silicon structure

  • High efficiency and sleek black appearance

Polycrystalline Panels

  • Made from multiple silicon crystals

  • Bluish appearance, slightly lower efficiency, more cost-effective

PERC (Passivated Emitter Rear Contact) Panels

  • Includes a rear passivation layer to improve light absorption and conversion efficiency

Half-Cut Panels

  • Cells are cut in half to reduce power losses

  • Improved performance in shaded or partially obstructed conditions

Bifacial Panels

  • Captures sunlight from both front and rear surfaces

  • Increases overall energy production

Smart Modules

  • Equipped with integrated monitoring, power optimizers, or microinverters

  • Enhanced performance and monitoring under varying environmental conditions

EGing Solar Product Datasheets

Key Features:

  • 440W power, 22.5% efficiency

  • Bifacial design with up to 20% rear-side gain

  • N-Type TOPCon cells for low degradation

  • Super Multi-Busbar (SMBB) for higher power output

  • Excellent low-light performance & PID resistance

  • IP68 junction box and durable aluminum frame

  • 30-year power warranty, 25-year product warranty

  • Operating range: -40°C to 85°C, 1500V system voltage

  • Low temperature coefficient: -0.29%/°C Pmax
