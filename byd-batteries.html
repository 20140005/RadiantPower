BYD Battery Storage Systems

BYD stands for Build Your Dream and is one of the world’s most established energy technology manufacturers. Headquartered in Shenzhen, China, BYD designs and manufactures its batteries entirely in-house and is recognised globally as the world’s leading producer of rechargeable batteries.

BYD’s Battery-Box Premium range is engineered for high-performance residential and commercial energy storage, offering exceptional efficiency, robust backup power capability, and off-grid support. Using cobalt-free Lithium Iron Phosphate (LFP) chemistry, BYD batteries deliver industry-leading safety, long service life, and consistent power output across a wide range of environmental conditions.

These systems are compatible with many leading single-phase and three-phase high-voltage inverters, making them a versatile choice for new solar installations and system upgrades.

📩 To learn more about BYD battery solutions, contact the Radiant Power team.

Why Choose BYD Batteries?

Proven LFP Battery Technology

BYD uses Lithium Iron Phosphate (LiFePO₄) chemistry, offering superior thermal stability, long cycle life, and enhanced safety compared to traditional lithium-ion batteries.

High Energy Density

Compact module design delivers high storage capacity in a smaller footprint—ideal for homes and commercial sites where space is limited.

Modular & Scalable Architecture

BYD Battery-Box systems can be expanded at any time, allowing users to increase storage capacity as energy usage grows.

Safety & Long-Term Reliability

Advanced protection features include temperature monitoring, over-charge and over-discharge protection, and short-circuit prevention—ensuring safe and stable operation over the system’s lifespan.

Battery-Box Pro Platform

The Battery-Box Pro range offers flexible configurations suitable for residential, commercial, and large-scale energy storage applications.

Integrated Battery Management System (BMS)

Each BYD system includes a built-in BMS that continuously monitors performance, balances cells, and protects the battery under all operating conditions.

Broad Inverter Compatibility

BYD batteries integrate seamlessly with a wide range of leading solar inverters and energy management platforms.

BYD Battery Product Range

BYD Battery-Box RES

Residential Energy Storage
Designed for home solar systems, the Battery-Box RES series offers modular expansion and flexible capacity options to suit varying household energy demands.

BYD Battery-Box Premium HVM

High-Voltage Modular Systems
The HVM series is ideal for higher-capacity residential and commercial installations, delivering high efficiency, long cycle life, and excellent scalability.

BYD Battery-Box Premium LVS

Low-Voltage Systems
The LVS range is a low-voltage solution suitable for residential and small commercial applications, offering simple installation and expandable storage.

BYD Battery-Box LV

Low-voltage battery systems designed for reliable residential and commercial energy storage with modular flexibility.

BYD Battery-Box HV

High-voltage battery systems engineered for larger commercial and utility-scale applications requiring higher capacity and power output.

BYD Battery-Box Premium LVS

4.0 kWh Modules | 8–12 kWh Systems

Key Features

  • Modular Stackable Design
    Simplifies transport, installation, and future expansion.

  • Flexible Capacity Range
    One LVS tower supports 1–6 battery modules, delivering 4 to 24 kWh usable capacity.

  • External Inverter Compatibility
    Designed for use with compatible low-voltage inverters.

BYD Battery-Box Premium HVM

2.76 kWh Modules | 8.3–66.2 kWh Systems

Key Features

  • Internal Plug-and-Play Connections
    No additional wiring required.

  • Expandable at Any Time
    Easily adapts to changing energy requirements.

  • High Power Output
    Suitable for demanding residential and commercial applications.

Each HVM system consists of 3–8 modules connected in series, providing 8.3 to 22.1 kWh usable capacity per tower.
Up to three HVM towers can be connected in parallel, delivering a total system capacity of up to 66.2 kWh.

BYD Battery-Box Premium HVS

2.56 kWh Modules | 5.1–38.4 kWh Systems

Key Features

  • Tool-Free Internal Connections
    Simple, clean installation with no additional wiring.

  • Highly Scalable Design
    Expand capacity as energy needs evolve.

  • High Performance Output
    Designed to support a wide range of residential and commercial applications.

Each HVS system includes 2–5 battery modules, delivering 5.1 to 12.8 kWh usable capacity per tower.
Parallel connection of up to three systems allows total capacity of up to 38.4 kWh.
