Our Services

  • Home
  • Our Services

1. Residential Solar Systems

Power your home with clean, renewable energy. We design and install solar systems that reduce electricity bills, increase energy independence, and add value to your property.
What’s included:
  • Custom solar system design
  • High-quality panel and inverter installation
  • Performance monitoring and maintenance

2. Solar Battery Storage

Store the energy you generate during the day to use at night or during peak pricing. Our battery solutions help you maximize savings, reduce reliance on the grid, and provide backup power during outages.
Benefits:
  • Energy storage for night-time use
  • Reduced electricity bills
  • Backup power during outages
  • Increased energy independence

3. Commercial Solar Solutions

Lower your business operating costs and improve sustainability with large-scale solar systems. We provide end-to-end solutions for offices, warehouses, and industrial properties.
Services include:
  • Commercial system design and engineering
  • Energy consumption analysis
  • Large-scale installation and maintenance
  • Performance monitoring

4. Solar Consultation & Custom Proposals

Our experts work closely with you to understand your energy goals and provide a personalised proposal. We outline system design, costs, and estimated energy savings so you can make a confident decision.
What we offer:
  • Free consultation
  • Property and roof assessment
  • Detailed, tailored proposals

5. Installation & Ongoing Support

Our CEC-accredited installers ensure your system is installed safely and efficiently. After installation, we provide ongoing monitoring, maintenance, and support to ensure your solar system performs optimally for years to come.
Services include:
  • Professional installation
  • System monitoring and maintenance
  • Expert customer support
