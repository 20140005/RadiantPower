JA Solar Panels

JA Solar is a global solar manufacturer recognized for reliable, cost-effective panels suitable for residential, commercial, and utility-scale applications. While not always the absolute highest-efficiency panels, JA Solar focuses on proven cell technology, durability, and long-term reliability.

Why Choose JA Solar?

  • High Efficiency – Converts a significant portion of sunlight into electricity, maximizing energy yield even in limited-space installations.

  • Advanced Cell Technology – Uses PERC, half-cell, and N-type silicon technologies to reduce power loss, improve shading tolerance, and enhance overall performance.

  • Durable & Weather-Resistant – Sturdy frames and high-quality materials withstand heavy snow loads, strong winds, and extreme temperatures.

  • Bifacial & Double-Glass Options – Panels capture sunlight from both sides and offer extra durability for harsh environments.

  • Smart Modules – Some models integrate power optimizers or monitoring systems for enhanced system management.

  • Certified & Trusted – Complies with IEC, UL, ISO, and other international standards.

  • Warranty – Offers 12–15 years product warranty for standard panels and up to 25 years product warranty / 30 years linear power warranty on advanced modules.

JA Solar Panel Types

  • Monocrystalline Panels – Sleek black panels with high efficiency.

  • Polycrystalline Panels – Cost-effective, blue-toned panels for budget-conscious installations.

  • Half-Cut Cell Panels – Reduces power loss and improves shading performance.

  • Bifacial Panels – Generate electricity from both front and back sides for higher yield.

  • Double-Glass Panels – Enhanced durability for extreme conditions.

  • Large Area N-Type Panels – N-type wafers improve efficiency and temperature tolerance.

Featured Models

1) JA Solar 440W

  • High power output (430–455W)

  • Half-cell technology for better efficiency and shading tolerance

  • 1500V system voltage, built for harsh climates

  • High conversion efficiency for maximum electricity generation

2) JA Solar 440W (54D41)

  • High module efficiency up to 22.0%

  • N-type bifacial double-glass design for durability and performance

  • Very low LID for stable long-term output

  • Excellent temperature coefficient (-0.30%/°C)

  • Strong low-irradiance response

  • Mechanical strength: 5400 Pa front / 2400 Pa back load resistance

  • 25-year product warranty & 30-year linear power warranty

  • IEC 61215 / IEC 61730, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001 certified
