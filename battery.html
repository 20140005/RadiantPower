Solar Battery

Why Add Battery Storage?

Solar panels generate power during the day, but most homes use more electricity in the morning and evening. A battery stores excess solar energy so you can use it when you need it most — not when the grid charges peak rates.

Radiant Power designs battery systems for Melbourne homes and businesses that want greater independence, blackout protection, and smarter control over their energy.

Power When It Matters

  • Reliable backup power during blackouts
  • Store excess solar for evening use
  • Quiet operation — no generator noise
  • Lower reliance on peak-rate grid electricity

Cleaner, Smarter Energy

By storing and using more of your own solar energy, you reduce reliance on fossil fuels and move closer to energy self-sufficiency.

On cloudy days or during low solar production, draw from stored energy instead of buying expensive power from the grid.

Key Benefits

Benefits of Solar Batteries

From backup power to lower bills, battery storage is one of the smartest upgrades for any solar-powered home or business.

Reliable Backup Power

Keep essential circuits running during blackouts so your home or business stays powered when the grid goes down.

Lower Energy Bills

Use stored solar instead of buying peak-rate electricity from your retailer.

Lower Carbon Footprint

Use more of your own clean solar energy and reduce reliance on fossil-fuel generated grid power.

Quiet Operation

Unlike generators, battery systems run silently with no fumes or neighbourhood noise.

Smart Energy Usage

Store daytime solar production and draw on it during evenings, cloudy weather, or high-demand periods.

Rebates & Incentives

Access available Victorian battery rebates and programs to reduce upfront costs.
