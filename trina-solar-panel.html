Trina Solar Panels

Trina Solar, founded in 1997, is one of China’s pioneering photovoltaic manufacturers and a global leader in solar technology innovation and production. With over 13,000 employees worldwide, Trina Solar has delivered more than 50 GW of solar panels globally and maintains a strong financial position as a publicly listed company on the Shanghai Stock Exchange.

Trina Solar panels are engineered for high efficiency, reliability, and long-term performance, making them an excellent choice for residential, commercial, and utility-scale installations.

Highlights of Trina Solar Panels

  • High Efficiency
    Utilizes advanced cell technologies including PERC and bifacial cells for maximum energy generation.

  • Reliability & Durability
    Minimizes micro-cracks with non-destructive cutting technology, ensures PID resistance, and is tested to withstand high winds and snow loads.

  • Industry Certifications
    Certified by IEC and other global standards, demonstrating compliance with safety and performance regulations.

  • Versatile Product Range
    Offers monocrystalline and polycrystalline panels with a variety of power outputs, suitable for all installation types.

  • Warranty Options
    Long-term warranties provide peace of mind, with details depending on the panel model.

  • Strong Global Presence
    Trusted by residential, commercial, and utility-scale customers worldwide.

Types of Trina Solar Panels

Vertex Series

  • Flagship series with high power density and advanced multi-busbar technology

  • Large wafer size for maximum efficiency

  • Ideal for residential and commercial rooftops

Honey Series

  • Includes mono and polycrystalline panels

  • Cost-effective and reliable

  • Uses PERC technology with a wide range of power outputs

Tallmax Series

  • High-power panels optimized for utility-scale projects

  • Robust performance in challenging environments

Duomax Series

  • Bifacial panels generating electricity from both front and back

  • Captures reflected sunlight for enhanced energy production

Tallmax M Plus Series

  • Upgraded Tallmax panels with higher efficiency and power output

  • Suitable for utility-scale installations and space-constrained systems
