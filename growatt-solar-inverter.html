Growatt Solar Inverters – Affordable, Efficient, and Reliable

About Growatt:
Founded in 2010, Growatt New Energy specializes in solar inverters and energy storage solutions for residential, commercial, and utility-scale applications. Headquartered in Shenzhen, China, with a 20GW annual manufacturing capacity in Huizhou, Growatt is recognized globally as a leading supplier of cost-effective, high-efficiency inverters. Growatt products have been exported to over 100 countries and are ranked top globally for residential inverters.

Highlights of Growatt Inverters

  • High Efficiency: Maximum efficiency ranges from 97.2% to 98.4%, maximizing energy yield from solar panels.

  • Wide Power Range: Inverters available from 1kW up to 2.52MW, suitable for any scale of installation.

  • Multiple MPPT Trackers: Optimizes energy harvest in shading or multi-orientation roof layouts.

  • Compact & Lightweight: Easy installation, less space required.

  • Monitoring & Communication: Built-in smart monitoring for real-time system tracking.

  • Grid Support & Safety: Anti-islanding, overvoltage protection, ground fault detection.

  • Reliable Performance: Designed to withstand harsh environmental conditions and ensure long-term durability.
